N.E.S.T.
National Employability Skills Test

N.E.S.T. is a proprietary assessment module which delivers both online and offline assessments for all Aspire Employability Products. It is designed to assess an individual on 10 different axes related to a person’s:

  • Personality and Soft Skills
  • General Aptitude and Reasoning
  • English Language and Communication Skills

Highlights

Online Testing

Aspire has the capability to deliver N.E.S.T. online for 100s of candidates simultaneously.

Configurable Axes

N.E.S.T. can be easily customized for multiple tests across 50+ axes like Personality and Soft Skills, General Aptitude and Reasoning and English Language and Communication Skills, etc.

Dynamic Benchmarking

N.E.S.T. compares every assessed candidate with his/her university, state, country, etc. to give a dynamic benchmarking and percentile score for real understanding of his/her competitive career positioning.

Employability Index

Finally, N.E.S.T. is unique in also comparing each candidate’s scores with an Employability Index, an Employer-driven dynamic, real-time index for skills in entry-level professionals.

Features

  • Questions of multiple difficulty levels
  • Questions in the form of text or image
  • Up to 5 choices for the answers to every question
  • Large question bank to ensure randomness
  • Automatic individual score analysis and generation of reports

Benefits

Candidate

  • Candidates can identify their strengths and development areas in relation to their goals
  • Objective pre-and-post training assessment for SWOT diagnosis
  • Assesses individuals in their listening skills, leadership, team work, time management, language and the seven management hats
  • Gives the candidates a clear idea of where they stand vis-a-vis the employability index
